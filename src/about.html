About TourEth

Who We are

TourEth is a site that provides the access to preview the countless and amazing tourist destination sites in Ethiopia. This site is developed and updated by a 3rd year Software Engineering Student at Addis Ababa Institute of Technology as part of a course project. Even though the site startd as a web projcet idea, the current main goal of TourEth is to widen the popularity of Ethiopia in the eye of tourists

History of TourEth

The idea of the website was concieved when the author was assigned to prepare a design for a web project. TourEth is currently in its early stage of development. Despite its infancy TourEth is showing early prospects of influence on exposing the heritages that the contry posseses, which was mostly not known by Tourists and the international community, even most heritages are taken for granted by people residing in the country.
